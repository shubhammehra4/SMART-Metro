Smart Metro


Social Distancing


Since we know sometimes how a particular station gets packed and overcrowded. Hence we have to wait for the next metro or even metro's to come and then move to our destination but now our project will tell you to go to a particular station depending on the crowds in the other stations.

Save Time


Each station is connected to the other station by not just one but by multiple paths. Since we know that the capital runs various metro lines namely yellow, pink, magenta etc. Our project will also tell you the best optimal path to reach your destination station from your source station, thereby saving the individual's time.
